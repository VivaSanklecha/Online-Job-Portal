Job Seeker

Job seeker is the any person who need the job. Job seekers can register their details on the web-site. After successful registration job seeker can login on the web-site. Job seeker can view details of companies which are registered on the web-site.

HireMe is a web application which connects Employer & Job seekers. Employers are the resources of the various jobs.

Job seekers can get appropriate job according to their various qualification. Employers have to register their company details on the site.

Job seekers should register themselves with their details on the site and upload their resume.

If the employer wants to interview the job seeker, he can select the job seekers on different criteria.

This invitation is sent to the job seeker via mail. The job seekers can apply for any job listed in their mailbox, this application confirms employer that job seeker is attending interview & other related processes.

Employer

Employer is the job provider. Employer can register their company on the web-site. After administrative permission, employer is able to login on the web-site. Employer can search appropriate candidate according to company requirement. Employer can appoint the candidates by sending mail to them.
